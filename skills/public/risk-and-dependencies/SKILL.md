# Risk and Dependencies

This skill is for when hidden coordination cost is the real problem.

## Default outputs
- RAID log
- dependency map
- risk review
- mitigation plan
- escalation summary
- assumption audit

## Workflow
1. List the initiative or workstreams.
2. Find coupling between teams, dates, and systems.
3. Separate risks, assumptions, issues, and dependencies.
4. Assign owners and timelines.
5. Flag items requiring escalation.
