# Quality Bar

Use this before finalizing any PM artifact.

## Clarity
- Is the main recommendation or status visible in the first screen?
- Can a busy executive understand the point in under 60 seconds?
- Are sections ordered by decision value, not process history?

## Rigor
- Are facts separated from assumptions?
- Are tradeoffs explicit?
- Are success metrics defined?
- Are major risks named with mitigations?

## Actionability
- Does the artifact make the next step obvious?
- Are owners / decisions / dates clear where needed?
- Could another team execute from this without a meeting to decode it?

## Concision
- Can 20-30% be cut without losing decision value?
- Did you remove generic strategy language?
- Did you replace adjectives with evidence?

## Credibility
- Are estimates framed honestly?
- Are open questions called out instead of hidden?
- Does the recommendation sound like it understands constraints and consequences?
