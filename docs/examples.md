# Example Transformations

## Example 1: weak status update

"Things are moving forward. The team is making progress and we are working through some blockers."

## Better

- **Status:** At risk
- **What changed:** Data pipeline validation slipped 4 days due to schema mismatch.
- **Top risk:** If unresolved by Wednesday, beta launch moves from June 12 to June 19.
- **Decision needed:** Approve temporary manual reconciliation path by Tuesday EOD.
- **Next milestone:** End-to-end dry run Friday.

## Example 2: weak launch plan

"Coordinate with teams and make sure everyone is ready."

## Better

- Product: final copy and UX signoff by May 20
- Engineering: release candidate cut by May 22
- Support: FAQ and escalation runbook by May 23
- Marketing: customer announcement draft by May 24
- Go/no-go review: May 27, 10:00 AM ET
- Rollback owner: Eng lead

## Example 3: weak decision memo

"We think option B is probably the best path."

## Better

"Recommend option B because it preserves the Q3 deadline while containing vendor risk to one interface. It increases short-term implementation complexity, but avoids a six-week dependency on the data platform migration now underway."
